Prerequisites

Before following this tutorial, make sure you have:

  • A working Route4Me account.

  • A working Twilio account (even if it's in Trial Mode).

  • A working Heroku account (even if it's in the Free plan).

  • Ruby and Ruby on Rails installed on your computer. Details about their specific versions is elaborated in the next section.

  • Git and the Heroku CLI installed on your computer.

  • Experience working with the command-line.
